üìç Copy your address

Use the wallet copy button to avoid typos. Most faucets require you to paste this address into a request form.

Step-by-step

  • Pick the network you want to practice on (e.g., Sepolia for Ethereum smart contracts or Solana Devnet for Solana programs).
  • Open the faucet link, paste your wallet address, and follow any verification prompts.
  • Wait for the transaction to confirm, then view the tokens in your wallet or on a block explorer like Etherscan/Solscan.
  • Use the tokens to run your lab: deploy a contract, send a transfer, or test a swap route.

Safety & classroom tips

Keep it separate.

Use a dedicated test wallet and email. Never mix seed phrases or private keys between testnets and mainnet accounts.

Watch daily limits.

Most faucets throttle requests. If a class needs more tokens, stagger requests or rotate among multiple faucets.

Verify networks.

Match RPC URLs and chain IDs precisely. Wrong network settings lead to failed transactions or missing balances.

Demonstrate explorers.

Ask students to open the transaction hash on a block explorer to reinforce transparency concepts.
